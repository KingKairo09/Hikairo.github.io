About Me

Kia Ora, Fakalofa Atu, Kia Orana & welcome I'm Hika – a Māori & Pasifika 3D Creator with a background in Game Design, Animation, and Film. I'm passionate about crafting captivating virtual worlds with Unreal Engine 5 and Blender.

My background in Film, Game Dev & Animation taught me how to work creatively, solve complex problems effectively, and stay on schedule - all while continuously learning new skills.

As a self-taught digital Creative, I’ve embraced these principles to refine my craft, driven by curiosity, creativity, and a dedication to immersive design. I enjoy transforming imaginative ideas into stunning visual experiences, from richly detailed environments to seamless looping animations and creatively dynamic concept art.
